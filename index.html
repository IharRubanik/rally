about

OneLife Rally is an unique event. Every year this carefully designed adventure is taking car lovers from around the world through sevencountries. Organization, service, reliability, innovation of the highest quality, reliability, and an international orientation have made OneLife Rally one of the leading rallies in the region.

When driving becomes a philosophy!

OneLife Rally NFT Collection brings blockchain technology adoption especially for you. The collection will contain 555 3D rendered artworks on the Ethereum Blockchain, each of them unique, with different colors, backgrounds, and elements related to the OneLife Rally philosophy. Each NFT will contain a car that you will be able to see & hear at the rally. Apart from that some pieces from the collection will give you the opportunity to enjoy crazy moments behind the steering wheel. Stay tuned for that!

Each year, we donate money for children. We believe they are the most valuable and vulnerable social category. Also NFT project will follow that vison. A part of the revenues from this collection will be directly donated to the kids in need.

What’s an NFT?

An NFT stands for “Non-fungible token” and is a fancy way of saying it’s a unique, one of a kind digital item that users can buy, own, and trade. Some NFTs main purpose is to be digital art. On the other hand, the NFTs from OneLife Rally offer additional utility like exclusive access to OneLife Rally Events, second NFT Collection, Fantasy play to earn game, Car Metaverse and our networking groups. Basically, think about OneLife Rally like a rare piece of digital art that also acts as a unique member card
